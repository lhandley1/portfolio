    ZeroWasteCook

    Details

    Problem

    University students often struggle with food waste and tight budgets, contributing to financial strain and environmental impact. Many students lack quick, reliable ways to identify leftover ingredients and plan meals that use what they already have. This increases avoidable waste and household food costs.

    Solution

    Leveraging Agile (Scrum) principles, I developed ZeroWasteCook, an AI-powered web application for intelligent recipe recommendations and effective food management. The app allows users to upload photos of their pantry or fridge, classifies visible ingredients using a fine-tuned PyTorch InceptionV3 (CNN) model, and then queries the Edamam Recipe Search API to surface recipes that use those ingredients while respecting allergies and preferences. The web UI provides expiry tracking, suggested shopping lists, and estimated monetary & environmental impact metrics to encourage sustainable choices.

    Problem
