    Projects

    ZeroWasteCook

    Save More 💰 | Waste Less ♻️ | Cook Smart 🍳

    ZeroWasteCook is a Django-based web app focused on helping students reduce food waste, save money, and make sustainable recipe choices. Leveraging AI-powered image recognition (with a fine-tuned PyTorch InceptionV3 CNN) and smart recipe recommendations from the Edamam Recipe Search API, it provides personalised recipe ideas, expiry date tracking, environmental impact estimates (CO₂ emissions per serving), nutrition insights, and calculates potential food waste savings in pounds (£), based on UK Aldi supermarket prices.

    Core Technologies: Django, PyTorch, Edamam Recipe Search API, SQLite, Pandas & Pillow

    ChatAdvisor

    As part of an Agile Scrum team of seven on the Innovative Product Development module, I co-developed ChatAdvisor, an AI-powered chatbot designed to assist students with learning difficulties by simplifying access to extensive university student support resources. My contributions focused on integrating Apache Lucene for efficient information retrieval, utilising Microsoft Azure AI Text Analytics for concise resource summarisation, and configuring OpenAI's GPT-3.5 Turbo for empathetic, context-aware responses. I also developed the chatbot's user interface (UI) and FAQ page, enhancing usability and helping students quickly find the support they need.

    Core Technologies: Java, Java Swing, Apache Lucene, Microsoft Azure AI Text Analytics, OpenAI GPT-3.5 Turbo
